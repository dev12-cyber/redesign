About Yash Projects Fabrication Co.

Welcome to Yash Projects Fabrication Co. - Your Premier Destination for Commercial Refrigeration and Kitchen Equipment Manufacturer in Mohali (Punjab)

Yash Projects Fabrication Co. is your go-to source for top-quality commercial refrigeration and kitchen equipment solutions in Mohali, Punjab, and beyond. Whether you're in the hospitality industry, food service sector, or any business requiring specialized refrigeration and kitchen equipment, we have the expertise and products to meet your needs. Explore our range of innovative solutions designed to enhance efficiency, reliability, and performance in your commercial space. With Yash Projects Fabrication Co., excellence is our standard.

Yash Projects Fabrication Co. is a leading provider of commercial refrigeration and kitchen equipment solutions, catering to businesses across various industries. With a commitment to excellence and a dedication to customer satisfaction, we have established ourselves as a preferred choice for businesses seeking reliable and innovative solutions.

Yash Projects Fabrication Co. offers a broad range of Commercial Kitchen Equipment for Hotels, Restaurants, Hospitals, Food Courts, Educational Institutions, Industrial Canteens, Take Away Outlets, Club and other food serving outlets along with planning, Designing CAD layouts, Exhaust Systems facilities, Maintenance, and Service. The firm's multidisciplinary approach offers clients comprehensive, cost-effective solutions to their most challenging situations.

Founded by Mr. Ashish Raj, who brings over 17 years of industry experience, Yash Projects Fabrication Co. is driven by a passion for delivering superior products and services. Our team of professionals is committed to understanding your unique requirements and providing tailored solutions that meet your needs and exceed your expectations.

At Yash Projects Fabrication Co., we believe in building lasting relationships with our clients based on trust, integrity, and quality. With our expertise and commitment to excellence, we strive to be your trusted partner for all your commercial refrigeration and kitchen equipment needs.
